Basic Font Information

Rokkitt is designed from the font forms of a number of distinctive geometric slab serifs, sometimes called 'Egyptians', of the late nineteenth and early - mid twentieth century. Rokkitt is designed specifically as a display font for headings and headlines, though it can also be usefull as an alternative to sans serif text fonts.

This release supports the following 
Unicode ranges: Latin, Latin-Extended

There are 3 Source files:

1. src/Rokkitt.sfd Original Source Files in postscript curves.
2. src/Rokkitt.ufo Source Files in UFO format.
3. src/Rokkitt.otf OpenType font format.

To contribute to the project contact Vernon Adams
